{%- comment -%} home/card.html — one standard magazine card for any content item. Used by the section grids and the featured secondary grid on the homepage. Since hacks, tools, and field notes are all `posts` now (news system, issue #337), the card discriminates by the item's first category (Hacks / Tools / Field Notes), not by `it.collection` (which is always `posts`). The `section` value maps to the cover-art key in home/cover.html.
